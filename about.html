About Three Star's English Boarding School

Established in 2061, Three Star's English Boarding School is a distinguished educational institution located in the vibrant city of Dhangadi, Nepal. With a legacy spanning over a century, our school has been a catalyst for academic excellence, character development, and community engagement.

Historical Legacy

Three Star's English Boarding School was founded in 2061 by visionary educators who sought to create a nurturing environment for learning. Since then, the school has been a cornerstone of educational innovation and a home to generations of scholars.

Academic Excellence

Our commitment to academic excellence is unwavering. The school boasts a distinguished faculty, cutting-edge curriculum, and state-of-the-art facilities. We empower our students to excel academically and cultivate a lifelong love for learning.

Community Engagement

Three Star's English Boarding School takes pride in its strong ties to the local community. Through various outreach programs, social initiatives, and collaborative projects, our students actively contribute to the well-being and development of Dhangadi and its surrounding areas.

State-of-the-Art Facilities

We believe in providing an environment that fosters creativity and innovation. Our campus is equipped with modern facilities, including advanced laboratories, a well-stocked library, and interactive learning spaces, ensuring a holistic educational experience.

Explore the beauty of our campus and witness the vibrant learning atmosphere at TSBSHKS.

Global Perspective

Recognizing the importance of a global outlook, Three Star's English Boarding School encourages international collaborations, exchange programs, and cultural exchanges. We aim to prepare our students for a diverse and interconnected world.

Future-Ready Education

As we look to the future, Three Star's English Boarding School remains committed to providing a future-ready education. Our students are equipped with the skills, knowledge, and values necessary to navigate the complexities of the 21st century.
